About New York

New York is one of the most iconic states in the United States, known for its vibrant cities, historical landmarks, diverse culture, and strong influence on finance, fashion, arts, and media. The state is home to New York City, one of the most famous metropolitan areas in the world.

From the Statue of Liberty to Niagara Falls, New York offers a unique blend of natural and modern attractions that draw millions of visitors every year.

Major Attractions in New York

  • Statue of Liberty
  • Times Square
  • Central Park
  • Niagara Falls
  • Empire State Building
