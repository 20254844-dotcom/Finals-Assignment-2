About Washington

Washington is known for its evergreen forests, mountains, volcanoes, and vibrant technology hubs. It is home to Seattle, Mount Rainier, and some of the most breathtaking landscapes in the Pacific Northwest.

The state blends nature and innovation, being the birthplace of companies such as Microsoft, Boeing, and Amazon.

Popular Places in Washington

  • Seattle Space Needle
  • Olympic National Park
  • Pike Place Market
  • Mount St. Helens
